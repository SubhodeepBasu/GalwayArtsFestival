Galway has only a few typical sightseeing spots - what makes it a wonderful place to stay are the atmosphere, the culture, the people, and the events. Although Galway is among the largest cities in Ireland, it has retained much of its lovely small town character.

If all the pub-crawling and festivities in the city feel too much for you, Galway has some breath-taking destinations around it. Day trips to the Aran Islands, Cliffs of Moher and Connemara are few of the most sought after destinations around the city. Enjoy exploring Galway!
