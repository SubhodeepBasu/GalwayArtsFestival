—How to get here—

—Travelling by Car—

Coming from the North (Donegal and Sligo): use the N17
Coming from the East (Dublin): use the M6
Coming from the South (Limerick, Cork and Shannon): use the N18

—Travelling by Public Transportation—

—Train—

The train station is at Eyre Square. The city is less than three hours away from Dublin and very well serviced by rail. Find out more here.

—Buses—

You can come to Galway with our public bus service, or with one of the private bus services like CityLink.

—Galway City Transport—

To get around the city, we recommend to take the bus or a taxi.

—Travelling by Air—

Ireland West Airport Knock and Shannon Airport are both a one hour's and Dublin International Airport more than a two hour's drive away from Galway. To get to Galway, choose the bus, train or hire a car.
