The comedy performances have always been some of the key highlights of the Galway Arts festival over the years. The programme has been structured in a way that creates an enriching amalgamation of local Irish talents and top standup comics from all around the word. The festival is centered around Eyre Square. So in case you need your funny bones tickled, you know where to go.
