—Festival—

The Galway Arts Festival (GAF) is one of the biggest cultural events in Ireland. It takes place each July in Galway, Ireland and covers a broad range of arts, from theatre, music, visual arts, comedy, rare arts to movies. Each year, the festival attracts more than 300,000 people from inside and outside Ireland to enjoy the presented arts and spent an amazing time in Galway. The Irish Times named the festival among the Top 3 art festivals in Europe. Come and see it for yourself!

—Galway—

Galway, a port city on the west coast of Ireland, is located where the Corrib River flows into the Atlantic Ocean. Galway's centre is the Eyre Square, a famous gathering place with shops and traditional pubs that offer live music almost every night. Nearby, cafes, art galleries and boutiques can be found in the Latin Quarter. Apart from the city, County Galway with its stunning views and hiking paths is definitely worth a trip.
