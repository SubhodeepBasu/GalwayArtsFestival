We need YOU to help make our festival happen!

Why volunteer?

Helping and volunteering is proven to make you happy! You can help us to make the festival happen and thereby bless all our festival visitors. And of course, you will also benefit from volunteering as it looks very good on your CV.

Who can become a volunteer?

Everybody can volunteer! Every age, gender and background is welcome. We have various tasks so we hail every skill set and helping hand. Just enter your name and mail address to sign up as a volunteer.
