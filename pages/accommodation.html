Each year, Galway welcomes thousands of visitors from all over the world. This has resulted in some top-class choices of accommodations for every preference. From 5-star hotels to fun hostels and cozy b&bs, Galway has it all.

Whatever the budget or the duration, you will have a warm and comfortable stay in this hospitable city. As the GAF is centred around Eyre Square, we have handpicked some hotels, hostels and B&Bs for you that are fairly close to Eyre Square.
